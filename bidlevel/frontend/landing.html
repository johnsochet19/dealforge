03 · Explainable by design

Proposal parsing pulls base bid, alternates, allowances, unit prices, exclusions and lead times — each with its original text, source page and confidence. Fields below the threshold are flagged needs review instead of quietly entering the leveling grid.

Override a classification and BidLevel learns the phrase — the correction persists across rebuilds and teaches the normalizer.

04 · Risk & award

The cheapest bid should not automatically win

Risk analysis flags outlier pricing, math errors, missing scope, unbalanced unit prices, long lead times, expired insurance and low-confidence extractions — each with severity, cost impact and evidence. Best-value scoring then subtracts open-risk penalties, so a low number with real exposure loses on the merits.

05 · Scope & honesty

What is deliberately not here yet

OCR / CAD / BIM
The pipeline runs on extractable text; a real OCR or vision service plugs in behind one seam and everything downstream works unchanged. Binary uploads are recorded honestly as “no extractable text,” never fake-processed.
Deterministic AI
Extraction is pattern rules with real confidence scores and full provenance — the auditable first version. An LLM layer can replace each function behind the same contracts.
Email delivery
Invitation emails and reminders are generated and recorded, not SMTP-delivered — the dispatcher pattern is the seam for wiring real delivery.
Auth & storage
Single-tenant demo today; document text lives in the database. Production wants token auth, object storage and background workers.
